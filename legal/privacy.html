Last Updated: July 2026

At JR Nation, accessible from jrnation.cc, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by JR Nation and how we use it.

When you register for an account using Google Sign-In to participate in our daily quizzes, we collect basic profile information provided by Google, which includes your name, email address, and profile picture. This data is used strictly for maintaining your quiz score history and displaying your active session status.

2. Log Files

JR Nation follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. All hosting companies do this and a part of hosting services' analytics. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Google is one of a third-party vendor on our site. It also uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to jrnation.cc and other sites on the internet.

  • Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website or other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. These technologies are used to measure the effectiveness of their advertising campaigns and/or to personalize the advertising content that you see on websites that you visit.

Note that JR Nation has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

We would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:
